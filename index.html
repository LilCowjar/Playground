Our Mission

A Place for Everyone to Thrive

Community Fields is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to providing accessible sports programs, safe play spaces, and community gathering areas for families of all backgrounds.

Since 2010, we've been the heart of our community — hosting youth sports leagues, family events, and creating memories that last a lifetime. Every dollar donated goes directly back into our fields, playground, and programs.
